Welcome to wormid.org

Our aim is to simplify the process of developing new approaches for labeling C. elegans neurons in volumetric images by providing a resource for tools and code for facilitating neuron ID and related data analysis tasks as well as a consolidated database of various imaging datasets from labs around the world. We hope that the diversity of these datasets as well as the tools and protocols we provide here will expedite the development of new algorithms for automatic identification of C. elegans neurons enabling new discoveries on the dynamics of the C. elegans brain.

We provide links to existing databases of labeled neuroPAL images stored in the Neurodata without borders (NWB) format on the Dandi Archive as well as code for pre-processing, neuron segmentation, curation, and identification of novel neuroPAL images. See our page on adding your own data for information on how to upload your datasets to Dandi and add them to this site.
